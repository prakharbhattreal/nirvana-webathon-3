WEB-A-THON 3.0

are you ready to code your way to glory? Join us for the ultimate showdown of creativity, innovation, and web wizardry!

Event Details

Showcase your creativity in our Frontend Development Competition — build, design, and host your own responsive website. Gather your team or come solo and make the web better — one line of code at a time!

  • Date: 26th August 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM – 05:00 PM
  • Venue: MPH 1

Rules & Eligibility

  1. Open to school students teams of up to 4 members.
  2. Participants must bring their own laptops.
  3. Internet access will be provided at the venue.
  4. Projects must be submitted before the deadline.
