About Us

Tech Geeks Club, in collaboration with the School of Computing, is organizing Nirvana Tech Fest 2025 on October 17 and 18. This two-day event will bring together students from various schools and colleges to celebrate technology, creativity, and innovation. The fest will feature a wide range of activities including hackathons, workshops, seminars, science exhibitions, coding competitions, and certification examinations. In addition to the technical events, departmental stalls will showcase innovative projects and ideas, while the evenings will be highlighted with a vibrant DJ night to foster networking and celebration.
